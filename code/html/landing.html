Welcome to the Japanese Online Self-Help Utility, or JOSHU for short. In Japanese, JOSHU literally means "assistant", or "tutor", which is what this website attempts to do to anyone interested in learning the Japanese language. JOSHU was first created by Nan Puthaaroon for her creative thesis as one of the Plan II requirements. Various students from UT Austin Japanese Program, including F.
